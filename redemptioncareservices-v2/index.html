Individual Lives, Individual Care
Redemption Care Services brings 20 years of experience in providing care for adults with learning disabilities, including autism, and mental health issues, with the associated challenging behaviour. We recognise that no two people are the same, so we provide person-centred, individually tailored care for people with Learning Disabilities, Mental Illnesses, Challenging Behaviours and Autism so that our service users can feel as independent and socially-inclusive as possible.

A Satisfying Career

Redemption Care Services is an innovative care provider for adults with learning disabilities, mental health issues, autism and related challenging behaviours. We're passionate about providing personalised care for our service users and supporting our colleagues. We are very keen to support diversity and equality of opportunity at work.

The pandemic has only highlighted to everyone else what already knew: our colleagues are heroes doing life-changing work. Every day they make a difference to people's lives, enabling them to enhance their skills, achieve independence and social inclusion and be part of their community.

Redemption are always looking for new heroes to join our team. If you are interested, please apply using the form below.
